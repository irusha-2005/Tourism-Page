Explore Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is a beautiful island with a rich cultural heritage. Explore the misty trails of Horton Plains National Park, the historic Trincomalee Harbour, and Arugam Bay Beach for surfing. Visit Delft Island for wild ponies and colonial ruins, and admire the intricate wood carvings at Embekka Dewalaya. Finally, relax on the golden sands of Mirissa for whale watching and breathtaking sunsets.
